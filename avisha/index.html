Rooms

You value privacy. So do we. The number of people sharing a room is limited in order to maximize personal space per person. White walls and dark accents – minimalist – is our style for the interiors of the rooms. All air-conditioned rooms come with twin-sized beds, refrigerator, individual study table and safety deposit box, and bathrooms en suite. Rooms are Cable TV ready.

Location

You can find us strategically located along Alvero Street. One Avisha is a stone’s throw away from Gate 2.5 of Ateneo, Shakey’s and McDonald’s – perfect for late night burger cravings or ice cream fix.

Robinson’s Easymart is also located on the ground floor to provide you with the essentials. LRT Katipunan Station is less than a kilometer away. There are also coffee shops, convenience stores, pizza parlors, nail and waxing salons, laundry shops, banks, and a gym that are all within a walking distance.
