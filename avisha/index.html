One Avisha Residences



One Avisha Residences is the newest student dormitory in Katipunan. We built it for students who want to live closer to school. We took into consideration all their preferences in choosing a home- away-from-home and matched it with the latest in terms of room design, layout, and security measures. We did all these because we want you to have the best college experience. So allow us to become your home!

Rooms

You value privacy. So do we. The number of people sharing a room is limited in order to maximize personal space per person. White walls and dark accents – minimalist – is our style for the interiors of the rooms. All air-conditioned rooms come with twin-sized beds, refrigerator, individual study table and safety deposit box, and bathrooms en suite. Rooms are Cable TV ready.

Amenities

  Feel like you’re staying in a hotel with our twin-sized beds, complimentary high speed WiFi, room intercom, refrigerator, microwave, study table (for those hell weeks!), safety deposit box, hot and cold shower. Cable TV can easily be installed. On top of that, there’s also a weekly complimentary housekeeping service to help you keep your room clean and orderly!

The building is fully covered with our CCTV cameras and 24/7 security. Residents are given access cards to secure and monitor entry in the building. In cases of emergency, the building has an emergency power supply. Residents can also enjoy the lounge area. In addition to this, there’s no need to go elsewhere due to our on-site laundry service, food outlets and minimart.

Security / Safety

  Dear parents, we know safety is your utmost concern. Do not worry about your children; they are safe in our facility. Our buildings are equipped with CCTV cameras while the round-the-clock security service will keep the peace. We also issue key cards to them to ensure they are the only ones who may enter the dormitory areas.

Location

You can find us strategically located along Alvero Street. One Avisha is a stone’s throw away from Gate 2.5 of Ateneo, Shakey’s and McDonald’s – perfect for late night burger cravings or ice cream fix.

Robinson’s Easymart is also located on the ground floor to provide you with the essentials. LRT Katipunan Station is less than a kilometer away. There are also coffee shops, convenience stores, pizza parlors, nail and waxing salons, laundry shops, banks, and a gym that are all within a walking distance.
