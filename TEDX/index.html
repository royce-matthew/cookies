What is TEDx

Imagine a day filled with brilliant speakers, thought provoking videos and mind blowing conversations

A TEDx event is a local gathering where live TED-like talks and videos previously recorded at TED conferences are shared with the community. TEDx Events are fully planned and coordinated independently, on a community-by-community bases. The content and design of each TEDx Event is uniquely and developed independently, but all of them have features in common.

By Attending a TEDx event, you participate in a gathering that will unleash new ideas, inspire, and inform
