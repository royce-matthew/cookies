About Me

A cum laude graduate of the UP ,Diliman Interior Design course in 1990, she is a registered PIID member since 1993. In 2002, Her masteral thesis on the Conservation of the San Agustin Monastery in Intramuros was completed at the Univ. de Salamanca , Facultad de Bellas Artes, Castilla y Leon Region of Spain with a grade of Sobresaliente. Her company ,helps Inarc Design Asia Ltd, HK. It is an interior design and architectural (SME) firm composed of 24 architects and designers. It had recently won design awards, in the Asia Pacific Region in 2012,2013 and 2014 . www.inarc.com She covers for them only business development for RP and Brunei.
